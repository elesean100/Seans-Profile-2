Wildlife Defenders Wanted!

We are looking for Hunters and Veterans to work as seasonal Wildlife defense troopers.

Every year, hunters are given a bad reputation for the practice of "Trophy Hunting".
At the same time, dozens of animal species are being destroyed by the illegal act of Poaching.
We are an organization that has set out to make a difference on both fronts.
In saving these animals, and changing the public vision of hunters.
We also are hiring Military Veterans looking to make a difference in the world, fighting a different kind of evil.

Job Description

You will be assigned to one of the many Wildlife reserves throughout Africa and will work with the local game wardens for a period of 3-5 months of the year in the following tasks:
1. Monitoring and Recording Wildlife numbers and activity
2. Aiding in the Investigation, tracking, arresting and if neccessary, dispatching of poachers
3. Assisting in the regulated hunts that will occur in the reserves


Job requirements

1. Candidates must pass a basic physical/mental evaluation
2. Have at least 5 years of Hunting/Outdoor experience
3. Pass a standard Marksman test.
4. No previous Wildlife centered charges/felonies.
5.Team worker, able to work and live with others.
6. No known allergies to anit-Malarial medications. ex: Doxycycline
